# Overview

To authenticate with Skio, you need:
1. **API Token** - A key that grants Nango permission to interact with Skio's APIs resources and services.

This guide will walk you through generating your **API Token** within Skio.

### Prerequisites:

- You must have an account with Skio.

### Instructions:

#### Step 1: Generating your API Key
1. Log in to your Skio dashboard.
2. Click the **API** button in the left-hand navigation bar.
3. Provide a name and generate your **API Token**.

#### Step 2: Enter credentials in the Connect UI

Once you have your **API Token**:
1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with Skio.
2. Enter your **API Token** in its designated field.
3. Submit the form, and you should be successfully authenticated.

<img src="/integrations/all/skio/form.png" style={{maxWidth: "450px" }}/>

You are now connected to Skio.
