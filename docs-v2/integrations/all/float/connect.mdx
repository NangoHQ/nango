# Overview

To authenticate with Float, you need:
1. **API key** - A key that grants Nango permission to interact with Float's APIs resources and services.

This guide will walk you through obtaining your API key within Float.

### Prerequisites:

- You must have an account with Float.

### Instructions:

#### Step 1: Finding Your API Key
1. Log in to your [Float](https://app.float.com/login) account.
2. Navigate to the [Integrations](https://nango.float.com/admin/api) page.
3. Scroll to the bottom of the page to locate your **API Key**.
<img src="/integrations/all/float/api_key.png" />

#### Step 2: Enter credentials in the Connect UI

Once you have your **API Key**:
1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with Float.
2. Enter your **API Key** in its respective field.
3. Submit the form, and you should be successfully authenticated.

<img src="/integrations/all/float/form.png" style={{maxWidth: "450px" }}/>

You are now connected to Float.
