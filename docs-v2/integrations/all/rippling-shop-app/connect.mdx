# Overview

To authenticate with Rippling Shop App, you need:
1. **Application Name** - The name of the application you would wish to connect to.

This guide will walk you through obtaining the **Application Name** within Rippling.

### Prerequisites:

- You must have a Rippling account and a registered integration in the Rippling Developer Portal.

### Instructions:

#### Step 1: Finding your Application Name
-   This is the name of the app you registered when creating an integration. 

#### Step 2: Enter credentials in the Connect UI

Once you have your **Application Name**:
1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with Rippling Shop App.
2. Enter your **Application Name** in its designated field.
3. Submit the form, and you should be successfully authenticated.


<img src="/integrations/all/rippling-shop-app/form.png" style={{maxWidth: "450px" }}/>

You are now connected to Rippling Shop App.
