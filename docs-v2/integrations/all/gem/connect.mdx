# Overview

To authenticate with Gem, you need:
1. **API key** - A key that grants Nango permission to interact with Gem's APIs resources and services.

This guide will walk you through obtaining your **API Key** within Gem.

### Prerequisites:

- You must have an account with Gem.
- The API must be enabled for your account before you can generate **API keys**. To request access, reach out to your Gem point of contact (Customer Success Manager or Account Executive).

### Instructions:

#### Step 1: Finding Your API Key
1. Login to your [Gem](https://www.gem.com/login) account.
2. Navigate to your **Team Settings**.
3. Go to the **Additional Settings** section.
4. Scroll to the bottom of the page to find the **API Keys** section.
5. In the **API Keys** section, click **Create API Key** to generate a new **API Key**.
<img src="/integrations/all/gem/api_section.png" />

#### Step 2: Enter credentials in the Connect UI

Once you have your **API Key**:
1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with Gem.
2. Enter your **API Key** in its respective field.
3. Submit the form, and you should be successfully authenticated.


<img src="/integrations/all/gem/form.png" style={{maxWidth: "450px" }}/>

You are now connected to Gem.
