# Overview

To authenticate with lemlist, you will need:
1. **API Key** - A key that grants secure access to the API, enabling authorized applications to interact with lemlist resources and services.

This guide will walk you through generating your **API Key** within lemlist.

### Prerequisites:

- You must have an account with lemlist.

### Instructions:

#### Step 1: Generating your API Key
1. Sign in to your [lemlist](https://app.lemlist.com/) account.  
2. In the left navigation menu, click on your profile, then select **Settings**.  
<img src="/integrations/all/lemlist/profile.png" />  
3. Navigate to the **Integrations** section and click the **Generate** button to create a new API key.  
<img src="/integrations/all/lemlist/integrations.png" />  
4. In the pop-up that appears, enter a name for your API key, then click **Create Key**.  
5. Your API key will be generated successfully.  
<img src="/integrations/all/lemlist/api_key.png" />  


#### Step 2: Enter credentials in the Connect UI

Once you have your **API Key**:
1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with lemlist.
2. Enter your **API Key** in its respective field.
3. Submit the form, and you should be successfully authenticated.


<img src="/integrations/all/lemlist/form.png" style={{maxWidth: "450px" }}/>

You are now connected to lemlist.
