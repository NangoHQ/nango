# Overview

This guide will walk you through retrieving your API key and connecting your JazzHR account using the Connect UI.

## Prerequisites
- You must have a registered JazzHR account.
- You must have access to the API key in your JazzHR account settings. Only Super Administrators, Recruiting Administrators, or Developers have visibility of the API Key.

## Step 1: Finding your API Key
1. Log in to your [JazzHR](https://app.jazz.co/app/v2/login) account.
2. Navigate to **Settings** > **Integrations**.
3. Locate the **API Key** section.
4. Copy your **API Key**. You will need this to connect JazzHR to Nango.

## Step 2: Enter credentials in the Connect UI

Once you have your **API Key**:
1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with JazzHR.
2. Enter your **API Key** in its respective field.
3. Submit the form, and you should be successfully authenticated.

You are now connected to JazzHR.
