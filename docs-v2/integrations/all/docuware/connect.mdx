# Overview

To authenticate with Docuware, you will need:
1. **Host Name** - The Hostname to your Docuware account.
2. **Username** - The username used to log in to your Docuware account. 
3. **Password** - The password associated with the above Docuware account.


This guide will walk you through finding your **Host Name** and credentials within Docuware.

### Prerequisites:

- You must have a cloud-based or self-hosted DocuWare account.

### Instructions:

#### Step 1: Finding Your Host Name
1. Login to your Docuware account. On you address bar, The URL will be similar to: `https://nango.docuware.cloud/DocuWare/Platform/WebClient`. Your **Host Name** is the text before `/DocuWare/Platform/`, which in this case would be `https://nango.docuware.cloud`.

#### Step 2: Finding Your Credentials

- These are the same credentials you use to log into your Docuware account.

#### Step 3: Enter credentials in the Connect UI

Once you have your **Host Name**, **Username** and **Password**:
1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with Docuware.
2. Enter your **Host Name**, **Username** and **Password** in their respective fields.
3. Submit the form, and you should be successfully authenticated.

You are now connected to Docuware.
