# Overview

Twilio uses API Key authentication. You'll need:
1. Account SID
2. Auth Token

### Prerequisites:

- You must have a registered account with Twilio.

### Instructions:

#### Step 1: Finding your Account SID

1. Log in to your [Twilio Console](https://console.twilio.com/)
2. Your Account SID is displayed in the homepage in the Account Info box

<img src="/integrations/all/twilio/account.png" />


#### Step 2: Finding your Auth Token

1. Your Auth Token will be displayed in the same box as the Account SID


#### Step 3: Enter credentials in the Connect UI

Once you have both the **Account SID** and **Auth Token**:
1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with Braze.
2. Enter the **Account SID** and **Auth Token** in their respective fields.
3. Submit the form, and you should be successfully authenticated.

<img src="/integrations/all/twilio/form.png" style={{maxWidth: "450px" }}/>

You are now connected to Twilio.
