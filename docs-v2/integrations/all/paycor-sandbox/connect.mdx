# Overview

To authenticate with Paycor (Sandbox), you need:
1. **Company ID** - Your Paycor (Sandbox) company identifier

This guide will walk you through finding your Company ID in your Paycor Sandbox environment.

### Prerequisites

- You must have a Paycor sandbox environment.

### Instructions

#### Step 1: Finding your Company ID
1. Log in to your [Paycor sandbox](https://hcm-demo.paycor.com/) account.
2. Click the â˜° menu icon, then navigate to **Company** > **Departments**.
<img src="/integrations/all/paycor/navigation.png" />
3. You'll find your **Company ID** in the top left corner. It will look something like `290180`. 
<img src="/integrations/all/paycor/company_id.png" />

#### Step 2: Enter your credentials in the Connect UI

Once you have your **Company ID**:
1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with Paycor (Sandbox).
2. Enter your **Company ID** in the designated field.
3. Submit the form to complete authentication.

<img src="/integrations/all/paycor-sandbox/form.png" style={{maxWidth: "450px" }}/>

You are now connected to Paycor (Sandbox).
