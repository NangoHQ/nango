## Setup guide

1. **Log in** to your [ClickSend Dashboard](https://dashboard.clicksend.com/).
2. Go to the [API Credentials page](https://dashboard.clicksend.com/account/subaccounts).
3. Copy your **Username** and **API Key**.

You will use:

- **Username**: your ClickSend account username.
- **Password**: your API Key.
