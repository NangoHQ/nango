# Overview

To authenticate with Open-Hands, you need:
1. **API Key** - A key that grants Nango permission to interact with Open Hands's APIs resources and services.

This guide will walk you through obtaining your API Key within Vercel.

### Prerequisites:

- You must have an account with Open-Hands.

### Instructions:

#### Step 1: Finding your API Key
1. Log in to your [Open-Hands account](https://app.all-hands.dev/).
2. Navigate to [API Settings](https://app.all-hands.dev/settings/api-keys).
3. Set the required permissions and create a new token.
<img src="/integrations/all/open-hands/tokens.png" />


#### Step 2: Enter credentials in the Connect UI

Once you have your **Token**:
1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with Open-Hands.
2. Enter your **Token** in its designated field.
3. Submit the form, and you should be successfully authenticated.

You are now connected to Open-Hands.
