# Overview

Planning Center Online supports OAuth 2.0 for integrations that are distributed to multiple churches.

If you have not created an OAuth app yet, follow [How to register your own Planning Center OAuth app](/api-integrations/planning-center-online/how-to-register-your-own-planning-center-oauth-app) first.

### Prerequisites

- You can sign in to Planning Center and access OAuth application settings for your organization or developer account.

## Connect in Nango

### Step 1 — Enter OAuth credentials in Nango

1. In Nango, open your **Planning Center Online (OAuth)** integration settings and enter:

   - **Client ID**
   - **Client Secret**
   - **Scopes** you configured on the OAuth app

### Step 2 — Authorize a connection

1. Go to [Connections](https://app.nango.dev/dev/connections) -> _Add Test Connection_ -> _Authorize_.
2. Complete the Planning Center authorization prompt and pick the organization to connect.

#### Notes

- PKCE is supported and recommended by Planning Center for OAuth authorization flows.
