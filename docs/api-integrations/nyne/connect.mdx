# Overview

To authenticate with Nyne AI, you will need:
1. **API Key** – A key that grants secure access to the Nyne AI API, enabling authorized applications to interact with Nyne AI resources and services.
2. **API Secret** – A key used in conjunction with the API key to authenticate your requests.

This guide explains how to obtain your **API Key** and **API Secret** for Nyne AI.

### Prerequisites

- A Nyne AI account with API access

### Instructions

#### Step 1: Getting your API credentials

1. Contact the Nyne AI sales team at **sales@nyne.ai** to request API access.
2. Once approved, you will receive your **API Key** and **API Secret**.

#### Step 2: Enter credentials in the Connect UI

Once you have your **API Key** and **API Secret**:

1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with Nyne AI.
2. Enter your **API Key** and **API Secret** in their respective fields.
3. Submit the form, and you should be successfully authenticated.

You are now connected to Nyne AI.
