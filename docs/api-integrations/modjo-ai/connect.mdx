# Overview

To connect with Modjo AI, you need your **API Key** from your Modjo AI account settings.

This guide walks you through finding it.

### Prerequisites

- You must have Administrator or Manager permissions in Modjo AI.

### Instructions

#### Step 1: Generate your API Key

1. Log in to your Modjo AI account.
2. Go to **Settings**.
3. Access the **Integrations** section.
4. Find the **Public API** card and click **Connect**.

<img src="/api-integrations/modjo-ai/integrations.png"/>

5. Create a new API key.

<img src="/api-integrations/modjo-ai/create_api_key.png"/>
<img src="/api-integrations/modjo-ai/generate_api_key.png"/>


6. Click **Copy** to copy your API key — make sure to save it, as Modjo AI does not store it and you will not be able to retrieve it later.

<img src="/api-integrations/modjo-ai/api_key.png"/>

#### Step 2: Enter credentials in Nango

1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with Modjo AI.
2. Enter your **API Key** in the respective field.
3. Submit the form to connect your Modjo AI account.

<img src="/api-integrations/modjo-ai/form.png" style={{maxWidth: "450px" }}/>

You are now connected to Modjo AI.
