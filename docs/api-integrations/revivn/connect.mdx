### Prerequisites

- You must have a Revivn account. [Sign up](https://www.revivn.com) if you don't have one.

### Instructions

#### Step 1: Get your OAuth2 credentials

1. Log in to the [Revivn App](https://app.revivn.com).
2. Navigate to **API** -> **API Credentials** -> **Create Application**.
3. Fill in the application name and submit. Your **Client ID** and **Client Secret** will be generated — copy both.

<img src="/api-integrations/revivn/credentials.png" style={{maxWidth: "450px" }}/>


#### Step 2: Enter your credentials in the Connect UI

1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with Revivn.
2. Enter your **Client ID** and **Client Secret**.
3. Submit the form, and you should be connected.

<img src="/api-integrations/revivn/form.png" style={{maxWidth: "450px" }}/>

You are now connected to Revivn.
