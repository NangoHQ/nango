# Overview

To authenticate with Pylon, you will need:
1. **API Token** - A key that grants Nango permission to interact with Pylon's APIs resources and services.

This guide will walk you through generating your **API Token** within Pylon.

### Prerequisites:

- You must have a Pylon account

### Instructions:

#### Step 1: Generating your API Token

1. Log in to your Pylon dashboard and navigate to your [API settings](https://app.usepylon.com/settings/api-tokens) page.
2. Generate your API Token.

#### Step 2: Enter credentials in the Connect UI

Once you have your **API Token**:
1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with Pylon.
2. Enter your **API Token** in the respective field.
3. Submit the form, and you should be successfully authenticated.

<img src="/api-integrations/pylon/form.png" style={{maxWidth: "450px" }}/>

You are now connected to Pylon.
