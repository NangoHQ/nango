# Overview

To authenticate with Reply.io, you will need:
1. **API Key** - A key that grants secure access to the API, enabling authorized applications to interact with Reply.io resources and services.

This guide will walk you through finding your **API Key** within Reply.io.

### Prerequisites:

- You must have a Reply.io account.

### Instructions:

#### Step 1: Finding Your API Key

1. Log in to your [Reply.io account](https://app.reply.io) and navigate to **Settings > API Key**.
2. Your API key is displayed on this page. Click **Copy** to copy it to your clipboard.

<img src="/api-integrations/reply-io/api_key.png"/>


#### Step 2: Enter credentials in the Connect UI

Once you have your **API Key**:

1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with Reply.io.
2. Enter your **API Key** in the field provided.
3. Submit the form, and you should be successfully authenticated.

<img src="/api-integrations/reply-io/form.png" style={{maxWidth: "450px" }}/>

You are now connected to Reply.io.
