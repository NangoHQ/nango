# Overview

To authenticate with Sellercloud, you need:
1. **Team Name** - Your Sellercloud team name.
2. **Username** - Your Sellercloud username.
3. **Password** - Your Sellercloud password.

This guide will walk you through finding your team name, username, and password.

### Prerequisites:

- You must have a Sellercloud account.

### Instructions:

#### Step 1: Finding your Team Name

1. Log in to your Sellercloud account.
2. Your **Team Name** can be found under your username in the top right corner. 

#### Step 2: Identifying your credentials

1. The **Username** and **Password** are the same credentials you use to log in to Sellercloud.

<Note>
Sellercloud recommends creating a separate user (employee) account for integration purposes. Use different user accounts for different integrations to improve security and manage API access more granularly.
</Note>


#### Step 3: Enter credentials in the Connect UI

Once you have your **Team Name**, **Username**, and **Password**:

1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with Sellercloud.
2. Enter your **Team Name**, **Username**, and **Password** in their respective fields.
3. Submit the form, and you should be successfully authenticated.

<img src="/api-integrations/sellercloud/form.png" style={{maxWidth: "450px" }}/>


You are now connected to Sellercloud.
