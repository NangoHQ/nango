# Overview

To authenticate with Salesmsg (PAT), you need:

1. **Personal Access Token** - A key that grants secure access to the API, enabling authorized applications to interact with Salesmsg resources and services.

This guide will walk you through generating your **Personal Access Token** from Salesmsg.

### Prerequisites

- You must have a Salesmsg account.

### Instructions

#### Step 1: Generate your Personal Access Token

1. Log in to your Salesmsg account.
2. Go to the [Personal Access Tokens](https://app.salesmessage.com/settings/developer/access-tokens) page.
3. Click **Create Token** to generate your unique Personal Access Token.

<img src="/api-integrations/salesmsg/create_token.png" />

4. Copy the token and store it securely—you won’t be able to see it again.

#### Step 2: Enter credentials in the Connect UI

Once you have your Personal Access Token:

1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with Salesmsg (PAT).
2. Enter your **Personal Access Token** in the credentials field.
3. Submit the form, and you should be successfully authenticated.

<img src="/api-integrations/salesmsg/form.png" style={{maxWidth: "450px" }}/>


You are now connected to Salesmsg (PAT).
