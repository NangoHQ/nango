# Overview

To authenticate with ShopVox, you will need:
1. **Email** - Your ShopVox account email
2. **Password** - Your ShopVox account password

Nango uses these to call the ShopVox login endpoint on your behalf and securely store the resulting `authToken` and `accountID` for all subsequent API calls.

### Prerequisites:

- You must have a ShopVox account.

### Instructions:

<a id="step-1-finding-your-credentials"></a>
#### Step 1: Find your credentials

Your email and password are the same ones you use to log in to your ShopVox account.

#### Step 2: Enter your credentials in the Connect UI

1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with ShopVox.
2. Enter your **Email** and **Password** in the corresponding fields.
3. Submit the form to complete authentication.

<img src="/api-integrations/shopvox/form.png" style={{maxWidth: "450px" }}/>


You are now connected to ShopVox.
