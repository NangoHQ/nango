# Overview

To authenticate with Ahrefs, you need:

1. **An Ahrefs account** – You will be redirected to Ahrefs to authorize access via OAuth.

### Prerequisites

- You must have an active Ahrefs account.

### Instructions

#### Step 1: Authorize Ahrefs

1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with Ahrefs.
2. Click **Connect** to be redirected to the Ahrefs authorization page.
3. Log in to your Ahrefs account if prompted.
4. Review the requested permissions and click **Authorize**.

You will be redirected back and your Ahrefs account will be connected.
