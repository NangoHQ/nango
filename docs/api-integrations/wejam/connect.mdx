# Overview

To authenticate with WeJam AI, you need:

1. **API Key** - Sent as the `X-API-KEY` header on every request.
2. **Bearer Token** - Sent as the `Authorization: Bearer` header on every request.

This guide will walk you through obtaining both credentials from WeJam AI.

### Instructions

#### Step 1: Getting Your API Key

1. Log in to your [WeJam AI](https://app.wejam.ai) account.
2. Navigate to your account settings or developer section.
3. Copy your **API Key**.

#### Step 2: Getting Your Bearer Token

1. In the same settings area, locate your **Bearer Token** (sometimes called an access token or personal token).
2. Copy the token value.

#### Step 3: Enter credentials in the Connect UI

Once you have both credentials:

1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with WeJam AI.
2. Enter your **API Key** and **Bearer Token** in their respective fields.
3. Submit the form, and you should be successfully authenticated.
