# Overview

To authenticate with Maximizer (On-Premise), you will need:
1. **Hostname** - The hostname of your Maximizer on premise instance

## Prerequisites

- A Maximizer on-premise instance.

### Instructions:

#### Step 1: Finding your hostname

- The **Hostname** is the domain name of your Maximizer on premise web access server. It is the part of the URL you use to access Maximizer, without `https://` and without any URL path.

#### Step 2: Enter credentials in the Connect UI

1. Open the form where you connect to Maximizer (On-Premise)
2. Enter your **Hostname** in the respective field.
3. Submit the form, and you should be successfully authenticated.

<img src="/api-integrations/maximizer-on-premise/form.png" style={{maxWidth: "450px" }}/>

You are now connected to Maximizer (On-Premise).
