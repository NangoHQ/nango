# Overview

To authenticate with ECU360, you will need:
1. **API Key** - Your ECU360 API key for authentication.
2. **Username** - Your ECU360 username.
3. **Password** - Your ECU360 password.

This guide will walk you through obtaining your **API Key** and setting up your ECU360 credentials.

### Prerequisites:

- You must have an active ECU360 account
- You must have access to your ECU360 account credentials

### Instructions:

#### Step 1: Finding your Credentials

Your **Username** and **Password** are the same credentials you use to log in to your ECU360 account. You'll need these along with your API key to authenticate.

#### Step 2: Obtaining your API Key

Contact the support team to obtain your API key. The API key is required to generate a session used to authenticate your requests.

#### Step 3: Enter credentials in the Connect UI

Once you have your **API Key**, **Username**, and **Password**:
1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with ECU360.
2. Enter your **API Key**, **Username**, and **Password** in their respective fields.
3. Submit the form, and you should be successfully authenticated.

You are now connected to ECU360.
