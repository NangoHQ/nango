# Overview

To authenticate with BambooHR, you will need:
1. **Subdomain** - The subdomain used to access your BambooHR account.

This guide will walk you through finding your **BambooHR Subdomain**.

### Prerequisites:

- You must have a registered account with BambooHR.

### Instructions:

#### Step 1: Finding Your BambooHR Subdomain

1. **Log in** to your BambooHR account.
2. Check the URL in your browser's address bar. Your **Subdomain** is the text that appears after `https://` and before `.bamboohr.com`.
   - For example, if the URL is `https://acme.bamboohr.com/home`, your **Subdomain** is `acme`.

#### Step 2: Enter Your Subdomain in the Connect UI

Once you have your **Subdomain**:
1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with BambooHR (OAuth).
2. Enter your BambooHR **Subdomain** in the corresponding field.
3. Submit the form to complete authentication.

<img src="/api-integrations/bamboohr/form.png" style={{maxWidth: "450px" }}/>

You are now connected to BambooHR (OAuth).
