# Overview

To connect with Agiloft, you need:

1. **Hostname** – The domain of your Agiloft instance.

This guide walks you through finding this value.

### Prerequisites

- An Agiloft account with access to the KB you want to connect.

### Instructions

#### Step 1: Find your hostname

Your hostname is the domain you use to access your Agiloft instance. For example, if you log in at `https://example.agiloft.com`, your hostname is `example.agiloft.com`.

You can find it in the browser address bar when logged in to Agiloft.

#### Step 2: Authorize in Nango

1. Open the Nango Connect form.
2. Enter your **Hostname** in the field provided.
3. Submit the form, and you should be successfully authenticated.

<img src="/api-integrations/agiloft/form.png" style={{maxWidth: "450px" }}/>

You are now connected to Agiloft.
