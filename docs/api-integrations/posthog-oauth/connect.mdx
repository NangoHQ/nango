# Overview

To connect with PostHog OAuth, you need:
1. **Subdomain** – The region prefix of your PostHog Cloud account (e.g. `us` or `eu`).

This guide walks you through finding your subdomain.

### Step 1: Finding your subdomain

Your PostHog subdomain matches the region your organization was created in:

| PostHog Cloud URL | Subdomain value |
|---|---|
| `https://us.posthog.com` | `us` |
| `https://us.i.posthog.com` (dedicated US) | `us.i` |
| `https://eu.posthog.com` | `eu` |
| `https://eu.i.posthog.com` (dedicated EU) | `eu.i` |

You can confirm your region by checking the URL in your browser after logging in to PostHog.

### Step 2: Enter your subdomain in Nango

1. Open the PostHog (OAuth) connection form in Nango.
2. Enter the subdomain value in the **Subdomain** field.
3. Click **Connect** and authorize access to your PostHog account.

<img src="/api-integrations/posthog-oauth/form.png" style={{maxWidth: "450px" }}/>

You are now connected to PostHog (OAuth).
