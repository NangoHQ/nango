# Overview

To authenticate with CleverReach, you will need:
1. **Client ID** - The OAuth Client ID from your CleverReach app.
2. **Client Secret** - The OAuth Client Secret from your CleverReach app.

This guide will walk you through creating an **OAuth app** in CleverReach to obtain your credentials.

### Prerequisites:

- You must have a CleverReach account.

### Instructions:

#### Step 1: Create an OAuth app

1. Log in to your [CleverReach account](https://www.cleverreach.com/).
2. Navigate to **My Account** > **Extras** > **REST API**.
3. Click **Create OAuth App** to register a new application.
4. Fill in the required fields:
   - **App Name**: Enter a name for your application.
   - **Redirect URL**: Enter your Nango callback URL (found in your [Nango integration settings](https://app.nango.dev/dev/integrations)).
5. Click **Save**.

#### Step 2: Obtain your credentials

After creating the app, you will see your **Client ID** and **Client Secret**. Copy these values.

<Tip>Keep your Client Secret secure. If you lose it, you will need to regenerate it.</Tip>

#### Step 3: Enter credentials in Nango

1. In Nango, go to your [CleverReach integration](https://app.nango.dev/dev/integrations).
2. Enter your **Client ID** and **Client Secret**.
3. Save the configuration.
4. Go to [Connections](https://app.nango.dev/dev/connections) -> _Add Test Connection_ -> _Authorize_ to test the connection.

You are now connected to CleverReach.
