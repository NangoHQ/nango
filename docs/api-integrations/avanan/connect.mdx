# Overview

To authenticate with Avanan, you will need:
1. **Subdomain** — The subdomain for your Avanan API region (see table below).
2. **Client ID** — Your Avanan Client ID from Avanan Support.
3. **Secret Key** — Your Avanan Secret Key from Avanan Support.

This guide will walk you through finding your **Subdomain** and obtaining your **Client ID** and **Secret Key**.

### Prerequisites:

- An Avanan account.

### Instructions:

#### Step 1: Finding Your Subdomain

You must use the correct subdomain for your region. In Nango, enter the full **subdomain** from the table below (e.g. `smart-api-production-1-us`).

| Region | Subdomain |
|--------|-----------|
| USA | `smart-api-production-1-us` |
| Europe | `smart-api-production-1-eu` |
| Canada | `smart-api-production-1-ca` |
| Australia | `smart-api-production-5-ap` |
| United Kingdom (UK) | `smart-api-production-1-euw2` |
| United Arab Emirates (UAE) | `smart-api-production-1-mec1` |
| India | `smart-api-production-1-aps1` |

#### Step 2: Getting Your Client ID and Secret Key

1. Contact [Avanan Support](https://www.checkpoint.com/harmony/email-security/) or your Avanan representative.
2. Request your **Client ID** and **Secret Key** for API access in your region.

#### Step 3: Enter Your Credentials in the Connect UI

Once you have your **Subdomain**, **Client ID**, and **Secret Key**:
1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with Avanan.
2. Enter your **subdomain**, **Client ID**, and **Secret Key** in their corresponding fields.
3. Submit the form to complete authentication.

<img src="/api-integrations/avanan/form.png" style={{maxWidth: "450px" }}/>

You are now connected to Avanan.
