# Overview

To connect with TalentLMS, you need:
1. **Subdomain** – The subdomain of your TalentLMS portal.
2. **API Key** – Your TalentLMS API key from Account & Settings.

This guide walks you through finding both values.

### Prerequisites

- You must have a TalentLMS account on a paid plan. API access is not available on the Free plan or during a Free trial.
- You must be an Administrator.

### Instructions

#### Step 1: Finding your Subdomain

Your subdomain is the part of your TalentLMS portal URL before `.talentlms.com`. For example, if your portal is at `https://acme.talentlms.com`, your subdomain is `acme`.

You can find it in the browser address bar when logged in to your TalentLMS account.

#### Step 2: Finding your API Key

1. Log in to your TalentLMS account as an Administrator.
2. Go to **Account & Settings**.
3. Select the **Integrations** tab.
4. Click on the **API** tile.


<img src="/api-integrations/talentlms/accounts_settings.png"/>

5. Enable the API if it is not already enabled.
6. Copy your **API Key**.


<img src="/api-integrations/talentlms/api_key.png"/>


#### Step 3: Enter credentials in Nango

1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with TalentLMS.
2. Enter your **Subdomain** and **API Key** in their respective fields.
3. Submit the form to connect your TalentLMS account.


<img src="/api-integrations/talentlms/form.png" style={{maxWidth: "450px" }}/>

You are now connected to TalentLMS.
