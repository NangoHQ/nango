# Overview

To authenticate with Clio, you will need:
1. **Hostname** - The hostname for your Clio region.

This guide will walk you through finding your **Hostname**.

### Prerequisites:

- You must have a Clio account.

### Instructions:

#### Step 1: Finding Your Hostname

Clio uses different hostnames based on your region:
- **US**: `app.clio.com`
- **EU**: `eu.app.clio.com`
- **CA**: `ca.app.clio.com`
- **AU**: `au.app.clio.com`

To determine your region, log in to your Clio account and check the URL in your browser's address bar.

#### Step 2: Enter credentials in the Connect UI

Once you have your hostname:
1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with Clio.
2. Enter the **Hostname** for your region (e.g., `app.clio.com`).
3. Submit the form and authorize the application when prompted.

You are now connected to Clio.
