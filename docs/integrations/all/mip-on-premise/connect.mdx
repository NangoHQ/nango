# Overview

To authenticate with MIP On Premise, you need four key pieces of information:
1. **HOST_REST** - The REST service URL for the on premise API service.
2. **YOUR_ID** - Your login ID for MIP Fund Accounting.
3. **YOUR_PASSWORD** - Your password for MIP Fund Accounting.
4. **YOUR_ORG** - The MIP Fund Accounting database you will be using.


This guide will walk you through finding or creating those credentials within MIP.

### Prerequisites:

- You must have valid login credentials for MIP On Premise.

### Instructions:

#### Step 1: Finding the HOST_REST variable
- This is provided in your enrollment materials

#### Step 2: Enter credentials in the Connect UI

Once you have the **HOST_REST ID**, **ID**, **PASSWORD** and **YOUR_ORG**:
1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with MIP.
2. Enter the **ID** in the designated field.
3. Enter the **Password** in the password field.
4. Enter the **ORG** in the org field.
5. Enter the **HOST_REST** in the HOST_REST Key field.
6. Submit the form, and you should be successfully authenticated.

You are now connected to MIP.
