# Overview

To authenticate with Bullhorn, you need:
1. **API Username** - The unique identifier assigned to your Bullhorn API account, used to generate a session that authenticates requests.
2. **API Password** - The secret associated with your API Username, used together with the API username.

This guide will walk you through obtaining your credentials from Bullhorn.

### Prerequisites:

- You must have an account with Bullhorn.

### Instructions:

#### Step 1: Obtain your API credentials
1. Go to the [Bullhorn Customer Hub](https://help.bullhorn.com/) and create a support ticket to request your API credentials.
2. Bullhorn will provide you with your **API Username** and **API Password**.

#### Step 2: Enter credentials in the Connect UI

Once you have your **API Username** and **API Password**:
1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with Bullhorn.
2. Enter your **API Username** and **API Password** in their respective fields.
3. Submit the form, and you should be successfully authenticated.

You are now connected to Bullhorn.
