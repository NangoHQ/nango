# Overview

To authenticate with Breezy HR, you need:
1. **Email** – The email address associated with your Breezy HR account
2. **Password** – The password for your Breezy HR account

This guide will walk you through finding your credentials within Breezy HR.

## Prerequisites

- You must have a registered Breezy HR account

## Step 1: Finding your credentials

Your Breezy HR credentials are the same email address and password that you use to log into your Breezy HR account.

## Step 2: Enter credentials in the Connect UI

Once you have your **Email** and **Password**:

1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with Breezy HR.
2. Enter your **Email** and **Password** in their designated fields.
3. Submit the form, and you should be successfully authenticated.

You are now connected to Breezy HR.
