# Overview

To authenticate with Devin, you need:
1. **API Key** - A key that grants Nango permission to interact with Devin's APIs resources and services.

This guide will walk you through obtaining your API Key within Devin.

### Prerequisites:

- You must have an account with Devin.

### Instructions:

#### Step 1: Finding your API Key
1. Log in to your [Devin account](https://app.devin.ai/).
2. Navigate to [API Settings](https://app.devin.ai/settings/api-keys).
3. In the **API Keys** section, click **View Key** to reveal your existing **API key**.
4. If you need to regenerate a key, click **Revoke and Regenerate**.
<img src="/integrations/all/devin/settings.png" />


#### Step 2: Enter credentials in the Connect UI

Once you have your **API Key**:
1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with Devin.
2. Enter your **API Key** in its designated field.
3. Submit the form, and you should be successfully authenticated.

<img src="/integrations/all/devin/form.png" style={{maxWidth: "450px" }}/>

You are now connected to Devin.
