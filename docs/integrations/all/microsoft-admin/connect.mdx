# Overview

To authenticate with Microsoft Admin, you need:
1. **Tenant ID** - The unique identifier for your organization that uses Microsoft services.

This guide will walk you through generating and finding your **Tenant ID** within Azure.

### Prerequisites:

- You must have an active Azure account with the appropriate Microsoft product subscription.

### Instructions:

#### Step 1: Finding your Tenant ID
1. Your Tenant ID can be found in the **Tenant ID** field on the [Overview page](https://aad.portal.azure.com/#view/Microsoft_AAD_IAM/ActiveDirectoryMenuBlade/~/Overview) within your Azure account. 
<img src="/integrations/all/microsoft-business-central/tenant_id.png" />

#### Step 2: Enter credentials in the Connect UI

Once you have your credentials:
1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with Microsoft Admin.
2. Enter your **Tenant ID** in its designated field.
3. Submit the form, and you should be successfully authenticated.

<img src="/integrations/all/microsoft-admin/form.png" style={{maxWidth: "450px" }}/>

You are now connected to Microsoft Admin.
