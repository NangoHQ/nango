# Overview

To authenticate with Fairing, you will need:
1. **Secret Token** - A key that grants Nango permission to interact with Fairing's APIs resources and services.

This guide will walk you through finding your **Secret Token**.

### Prerequisites:

- You must have a Fairing account.

### Instructions:

#### Step 1: Finding Your Secret Token
1. Login to your Fairing account.
2. Go to **Settings** > **API Credentials**.
3. Click the reveal icon to reveal your **Secret Token**.

- At the time of writing this guide, Fairing's API is still in beta. If you don't see the **API Keys** section in your account page, please reach out to the team.

<img src="/integrations/all/fairing/settings.png" style={{maxWidth: "450px" }}/>

#### Step 2: Enter credentials in the Connect UI

Once you have your Secret Token:
1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with Fairing.
2. Enter your **Secret Token** in its designated field.
3. Submit the form, and you should be successfully authenticated.

<img src="/integrations/all/fairing/form.png" style={{maxWidth: "450px" }}/>

You are now connected to Fairing.
