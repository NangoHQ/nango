# Overview

To authenticate with Icypeas, you will need:

1. **API Key** - A key that grants Nango secure access to interact with the Icypeas API.

This guide will walk you through finding your **API Key** within Icypeas.

### Prerequisites:

- You must have an account with Icypeas.

### Instructions:

#### Step 1: Finding Your Icypeas API Key

1. Log in to your Icypeas account and navigate to the [API Page](https://app.icypeas.com/bo/api).
<img src="/integrations/all/icypeas/api_settings.png"/>
2. Toggle the **Activate API access** button if it's not enabled.
3. Locate your **API Key** on this page.

#### Step 2: Enter credentials in the Connect UI

Once you have your **API Key**:

1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with Icypeas.
2. Enter the **API Key** in its respective field.
3. Submit the form, and you should be successfully authenticated.

<img src="/integrations/all/icypeas/form.png" style={{maxWidth: "450px" }}/>


You are now connected to Icypeas.
