# Overview

To authenticate with DocuWare, you will need:
1. **Domain** - The domain to your DocuWare account.
2. **Username** - The username used to log in to your DocuWare account.
3. **Password** - The password associated with the above DocuWare account.


This guide will walk you through finding your **Domain** and credentials within DocuWare.

### Prerequisites:

- You must have a cloud-based or self-hosted DocuWare account.

### Instructions:

#### Step 1: Finding Your Domain
1. Log in to your DocuWare account. On your address bar, the URL will be similar to: `https://nango.docuware.cloud/DocuWare/Platform/WebClient`. Your **Domain** is the text before `/DocuWare/Platform/`, which in this case would be `https://nango.docuware.cloud`.

#### Step 2: Finding Your Credentials

- These are the same credentials you use to log into your DocuWare account.

#### Step 3: Enter credentials in the Connect UI

Once you have your **Domain**, **Username** and **Password**:
1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with DocuWare.
2. Enter your **Domain**, **Username** and **Password** in their respective fields.
3. Submit the form, and you should be successfully authenticated.

You are now connected to DocuWare.
