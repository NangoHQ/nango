# Overview

To authenticate with Valley, you will need:
1. **Email** - Your Valley account email address.
2. **Password** - Your Valley account password.

This guide will walk you through connecting to Valley using these credentials.

### Prerequisites:

- You must have a Valley account.

### Instructions:

#### Step 1: Getting Your Credentials

Use the same email address and password you use to log in to your Valley account. 

#### Step 2: Enter credentials in the Connect UI

Once you have your **Email** and **Password**:
1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with Valley.
2. Enter your **Email** and **Password** in their designated fields.
3. Submit the form, and you should be successfully authenticated.

You are now connected to Valley.
