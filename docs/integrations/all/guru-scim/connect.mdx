# Overview

To authenticate with Guru (SCIM), you need:
1. **SCIM Username** - Your SCIM username for your Guru account.
2. **SCIM Token** - Your SCIM token for your Guru account

This guide will walk you through finding or creating those credentials within Guru.

### Prerequisites:

- You must set up an administrator account with Guru.

#### Step 1: Finding Guru SCIM Credentials

1. Log into your Guru account.  
2. Navigate to **Team Settings** > **Apps and Integrations** > **SSO/SCIM** tab.  
3. Ensure SSO is enabled. SCIM provisioning can only be turned on after enabling SSO.  
4. Turn on the **Authorize SCIM Provisioning** switch.  
5. Note the generated SCIM credentials,**SCIM Username** and **SCIM Token**.
<img src="/integrations/all/guru-scim/guru_scim.png" />

#### Step 2: Enter credentials in the Connect UI

Once you have your **SCIM Username** and **SCIM Token**:
1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with Guru (SCIM).
2. Enter your **SCIM Username** and **SCIM Token** in their respective fields.
3. Submit the form, and you should be successfully authenticated.

<img src="/integrations/all/guru-scim/form.png" style={{maxWidth: "450px" }}/>

You are now connected to Guru (SCIM).
