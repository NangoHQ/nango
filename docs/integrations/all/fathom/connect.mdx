# Overview

To authenticate with Fathom, you will need:

1. **API Key** - A key that is used to identify your Fathom account.

This guide will walk you through finding these Keys within Fathom.

### Prerequisites:

- You must have a registered Fathom account.

### Instructions:

#### Step 1: Finding Your API Key

1. **Log in** to your Fathom dashboard.
2. Click **Settings** in the top navigation bar.
3. Scroll down to **API Access** in the **Integrations** section.
4. Create a new API key.

#### Step 2: Enter API Key in the Connect UI

Once you have your **API Key**:

1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with Fathom.
2. Enter your **API Key** in the designated field.
3. Submit the form, and you should be successfully authenticated.

<img src="/integrations/all/fathom/form.png" style={{maxWidth: "450px" }}/>

You are now connected to Fathom.
