# Overview

Planning Center Online supports OAuth 2.0 for integrations that are distributed to multiple churches.

This guide walks through registering an OAuth application and connecting it to Nango.

### Prerequisites

- You can sign in to Planning Center and access OAuth application settings for your organization/developer account.

## OAuth app setup

### Step 1 — Create an OAuth application

1. Open Planning Center OAuth applications: `https://api.planningcenteronline.com/oauth/applications`
2. Create a new OAuth application.
3. Set the redirect URL for Nango:

   - **Redirect URI**: `https://api.nango.dev/oauth/callback`

4. Copy the **Client ID** and **Client secret** — you’ll paste these into your Nango integration settings.

### Step 2 — Configure scopes

Scopes map to Planning Center products (People, Services, Calendar, etc.). Choose the scopes your integration needs.

Official scope list:

- [Planning Center authentication docs — Application Scopes](https://api.planningcenteronline.com/docs/overview/authentication)

### Step 3 — Connect in Nango

1. In Nango, open your **Planning Center Online (OAuth)** integration settings and enter:

   - **Client ID**
   - **Client Secret**
   - **Scopes** you configured on the OAuth app

2. Go to **Connections** and authorize a new connection.

#### Notes

- PKCE is supported/recommended by Planning Center for OAuth authorization flows.
