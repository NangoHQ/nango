# Overview

To authenticate with Pendo using OAuth (`client_credentials` grant), you need two pieces of information:
1. **Client ID** - Your Pendo OAuth client ID
2. **Client Secret** - Your Pendo OAuth client secret


This guide will walk you through finding or creating those credentials within Pendo.

### Prerequisites:

- You must set up an account with Pendo.
- OAuth `client_credentials` access is currently part of an early-release Pendo capability and is not yet covered by Pendo's public documentation. To obtain credentials, contact your Pendo account team and request a **Client ID** and **Client Secret** for machine-to-machine (service account) access.

#### Step 1: Finding your Pendo OAuth credentials
1. Reach out to your Pendo account team / support and request OAuth `client_credentials` access for your subscription.
2. Pendo will provide you with a **Client ID** and **Client Secret**.
3. Securely capture and store the values — the client secret will not be shown again.

#### Step 2: Connect Your Pendo Account

To authenticate using your OAuth credentials, navigate to the Pendo (OAuth) authentication form in Nango.
Enter your credentials as follows:
- Client ID: Your Pendo OAuth client ID
- Client Secret: Your Pendo OAuth client secret

Click "Connect" to complete the authentication. Nango will exchange these credentials for an access token using the
`https://app.pendo.io/oauth/v1/token` endpoint with the `read:me` scope and the `client_credentials` grant type.
