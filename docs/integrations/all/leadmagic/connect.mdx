# Overview

To authenticate with LeadMagic, you will need:
1. **API Key** - A key that grants Nango permission to interact with LeadMagic's API resources and services.

This guide will walk you through finding your API Key within LeadMagic.

### Prerequisites:

- You must have a registered LeadMagic account.

### Instructions:

#### Step 1: Finding Your API Key

1. Log in to your LeadMagic account and navigate to the [API Key](https://app.leadmagic.io/settings/api) tab in the settings page.
<img src="/integrations/all/leadmagic/api_settings.png" />  
2. Locate your **API Key** on this page.


#### Step 2: Enter credentials in the Connect UI

Once you have the **API Key**:
1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with LeadMagic.
2. Enter your **API Key** in its designated field.
3. Submit the form, and you should be successfully authenticated.


<img src="/integrations/all/leadmagic/form.png" style={{maxWidth: "450px" }}/>

You are now connected to LeadMagic.
