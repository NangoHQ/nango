# Overview

To authenticate with Totango, you need:
1. **App Token** - A personal access token that grants Nango permission to interact with Totango's API resources and services.
2. **Service ID** - A unique identifier for your Totango service.

This guide will walk you through obtaining your App Token and Service ID within Totango.

### Prerequisites:

- You must have an account with Totango.

### Instructions:

#### Step 1: Finding Your App Token
1. Log in to your [Totango](https://app.totango.com/) account.
2. Follow instructions [here](https://support.totango.com/hc/en-us/articles/203036939-Personal-Access-Token-and-Service-ID) to get app token and service ID.

#### Step 2: Enter credentials in the Connect UI

Once you have your **App Token** and **Service ID**:
1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with Totango.
2. Enter your **App Token** in the App Token field.
3. Enter your **Service ID** in the Service ID field.
4. Submit the form, and you should be successfully authenticated.

You are now connected to Totango.
