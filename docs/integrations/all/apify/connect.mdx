# Overview

To authenticate with Apify, you need:
1. **API token** - A token that grants Nango permission to interact with Apify's API resources and services.

This guide will walk you through generating this within Apify.

### Prerequisites:

- You must have an account with Apify.

### Instructions:

#### Step 1: Generating Your API token

1. Log in to your Apify account and navigate to [API & Integrations](https://console.apify.com/settings/integrations).
3. Click **+ Create a new token**.
<img src="/integrations/all/apify/api_tokens.png"/>

4. Fill in all the required information and click **Create** to generate your API token.

<img src="/integrations/all/apify/create_token.png"/>


#### Step 2: Enter credentials in the Connect UI

Once you have your **API token**:
1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with Apify.
2. Enter your **API Token** in its respective field.
3. Submit the form, and you should be successfully authenticated.

<img src="/integrations/all/apify/form.png" style={{maxWidth: "450px" }}/>

You are now connected to Apify.
