# Overview

To authenticate with Streak, you need:
1. **API Key** - A key that grants Nango permission to interact with Streak's API resources and services.

This guide will walk you through generating your API Key within Streak.

### Prerequisites:

- You must have a Streak account.
- The Streak extension must be installed in your Gmail account.

### Instructions:

#### Step 1: Generating Your API Key

1. Navigate to Streak's [home page](https://mail.google.com/mail/u/0/#streak/home) and click the **Integrations & Automations** button at the top.
<img src="/integrations/all/streak/home_page.png"/>
2. Select **Custom integrations**.
<img src="/integrations/all/streak/custom_integrations.png"/>
3. Click the **+ Create new key** button to generate an **API Key**.
<img src="/integrations/all/streak/create_api_key.png"/>

#### Step 2: Enter credentials in the Connect UI

Once you have your **API Key**:
1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with Streak.
2. Enter your **API Key** in its designated field.
3. Submit the form, and you should be successfully authenticated.

<img src="/integrations/all/streak/form.png" style={{maxWidth: "450px" }}/>

You are now connected to Streak.
