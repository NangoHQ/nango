# Overview

To authenticate with Rocketlane, you will need:
1. **API Key** â€” A key that grants Nango permission to interact with Rocketlane's API resources and services.

This guide will walk you through generating your API Key within Rocketlane.

### Prerequisites:

- You must have a registered Rocketlane account with permission to generate an **API Key**.

### Instructions:

#### Step 1: Generating Your API Key
1. Log in to your Rocketlane account, click on your profile, then click **Settings**.
<img src="/integrations/all/rocketlane/profile.png" />

2. Navigate to **API**, click on the **Create API Key** button, enter a name, and click **Add**.
<img src="/integrations/all/rocketlane/create_api_key.png" />
<img src="/integrations/all/rocketlane/generated_api_key.png" />

<Tip>Once your **API Key** is generated, make sure to copy it immediately, as it will be displayed only once.</Tip>

#### Step 2: Enter credentials in the Connect UI

Once you have the **API Key**:
1. Open the form where you need to authenticate with Rocketlane.
2. Enter your **API Key** in its designated field.
3. Submit the form, and you should be successfully authenticated.

<img src="/integrations/all/rocketlane/form.png" style={{maxWidth: "450px" }}/>


You are now connected to Rocketlane.
